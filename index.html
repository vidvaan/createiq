About Us

Create IQ Technologies is a premier training institute offering high-quality courses in Java Full Stack Development, Python Full Stack Development, MERN Full Stack Development, and much more.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements by offering high-quality, practical, and hands-on training. Whether you're a beginner looking to enter the tech field or a professional aiming to upgrade your skills, Create IQ Technologies offers specialized courses tailored to meet the diverse needs of our students.

Why Choose Us?

  • Industry-Relevant Curriculum: Our courses are designed by industry experts to ensure you learn the latest technologies and best practices.
  • Experienced Trainers: We have a team of experienced trainers with hands-on experience in various fields of technology, who are passionate about helping students succeed.
  • Real-World Projects: We emphasize practical learning through real-world projects, allowing students to apply their knowledge and gain valuable experience.
  • Flexible Learning Options: With both online and in-person training options, you can learn at your own pace and convenience.
  • Job Assistance: We provide job assistance, including resume building, interview preparation, and placement support to help you kick-start your career in technology.
